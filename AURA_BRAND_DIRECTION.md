# AURA RESET STUDIO Brand Direction

## Core Concept

AURA RESET STUDIO is a contemporary reset studio born in Las Palmas de Gran Canaria.

Territory: reset rituals by the Atlantic.

Aura combines soft movement, breath, journaling and sensory in-person experiences for people with demanding lives who need to lower the noise, return to the body and continue with more clarity.

## Voice

All visible website copy is Spanish for the MVP.

The tone is close, editorial, serene, warm and direct. It should feel emotionally real without becoming clinical, spiritual cliché or generic wellness marketing.

Avoid medical claims, productivity claims, fake urgency, exaggerated transformation language and empty phrases such as "encuentra tu equilibrio".

## Visual Identity

Palette:

- Cal White: `#F7F3EB`
- Arena Canarias: `#DDC9AD`
- Atlantic Teal: `#438E91`
- Deep Atlantic: `#173E40`
- Sun Halo: `#F0B45A`
- Sunset Coral: `#E9785B`
- Lava Ink: `#252525`
- Soft White: `#FCFAF6`

Atlantic Teal is the recognisable Aura color. Use it in CTAs, details, hover states and visual accents. Do not use generic sage or olive as the main identity.

## Atlantic Halo

Aura uses a graphic element called Atlantic Halo: an organic circle of light inspired by sun reflection on Atlantic water. It combines Sun Halo, Sunset Coral and Atlantic Teal in a restrained way.

It should feel contemporary and ownable, not mystical or decorative for its own sake.

## Imagery

Use final local assets when available. The current MVP can use carefully selected temporary stock photography while the Aura shoot is pending.

Image direction:

- Las Palmas light and Atlantic water.
- Soft movement, breath, journaling and lived-in objects.
- Real skin, textiles, ceramic, glass, stone and natural surfaces.
- Small groups, never crowds.
- Boutique, sensory and local, without tourist postcard clichés.

Avoid aggressive fitness visuals, generic influencer poses, spiritual props, glossy luxury clichés and AI-looking portraits. Temporary stock should feel peaceful, Atlantic, volcanic, soft and human.
